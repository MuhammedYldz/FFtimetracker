FocusFlow

Delete your account and data

FocusFlow (developed by getpp) lets you delete your data at any time. This page explains how, as required by Google Play.

Delete data inside the app

  • Delete any individual time entry, task, or category from within the app.
  • Signing out removes your account's data from that device.

Delete your entire account and all cloud data

To permanently delete your FocusFlow account and all associated data stored in the cloud (your email/login and all synced time entries, tasks, and categories):

Email info@getpp.net from the address you signed up with, with the subject "Delete my FocusFlow account".

We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of the request. No data is retained after deletion, and none is kept for any other purpose.

What is deleted

Your login (email) and all time entries, tasks, and categories synced to your account. Integration access tokens you entered are stored only on your device and are removed when you sign out or uninstall the app.
